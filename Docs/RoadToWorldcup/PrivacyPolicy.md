# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

The King: Road to Champion is developed by Pham Duc Kien. This policy explains how the game handles information.

## Information We Collect

The game does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. It does not require an account, use advertising, track users across apps or websites, or use third-party analytics.

Game progress, virtual currency, and cosmetic selections are stored locally on the player's device. Removing the app or clearing its local data may remove this progress.

## In-App Purchases

The game offers optional consumable purchases. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store payment-card details or Apple Account credentials.

## Children's Privacy

The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions or support, please open an issue at https://github.com/nghienvothuat-a11y/RoadToWorldcup/issues.
